🔧 DEVELOPER CONSOLE ACCESS
============================

To access developer stats and live site analytics:

1. Login with email: tanu.shirke06@gmail.com

2. Developer Console will appear in the bottom-right corner of the screen

3. Features:
   - 👥 Total Users Online (Hirers + Students)
   - 📋 Total Open Tasks
   - ✅ Total Allotted Tasks
   - 📤 Total Submitted Tasks
   - 🏆 Total Completed Tasks
   - 💰 Total Transaction Amount
   - 📊 Live Task Statistics

4. Open Browser Console (F12 or right-click → Inspect → Console tab) to view:
   - Timestamp of last update
   - Detailed task list
   - All live platform metrics

The stats auto-refresh every 5 seconds!
